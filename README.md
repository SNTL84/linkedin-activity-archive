## 🎯 What This Is

A **complete LinkedIn activity archive and analytics system** for professionals, content creators, and agencies who need a structured, searchable record of their LinkedIn presence.

Stop losing your best content to the LinkedIn algorithm. This captures everything — posts, reposts, media, engagement data — and organizes it for reuse, audits, and strategy.

---

## 📊 What's Archived

| Data Type | Details |
|---|---|
| ✍️ Original Posts | Long-form articles, short posts, thought leadership |
| 🔄 Reposts & Shares | Curated content with context notes |
| 🎥 Media Links | Images, videos, embedded assets |
| 📈 Engagement Analytics | Likes, comments, impressions over time |
| 💻 Content Metadata | Tags, topics, post timing, performance scores |
